# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard-2025-in-Excel
A clean and interactive Excel dashboard designed to visualize coffee shop sales performance and help business owners make data-driven decisions.

![Capture6](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c6b59c02-cd54-4799-a842-280b146d519b)

### Key Features :
- KPI Cards: Display Total Revenue, Total Transactions, Average Order Value, and Peak Hour in real time.
- Monthly & Daily Sales Trends: Track revenue growth and identify high-performing days.
- Hourly Sales Insights: Discover the most profitable hours of the day.
- Top 5 Products by Revenue: Highlight the best-performing coffee products.
- Revenue by Time of Day: Visualize sales distribution across Morning, Afternoon, and Night.
- Best-Selling Product Card: Automatically shows the top product with its revenue percentage.
- Interactive Slicers: Filter data by Product Type or Time of Day for deeper insights.
